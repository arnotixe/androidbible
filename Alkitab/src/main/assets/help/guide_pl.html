(ENGLISH: Swipe in from left, Ajustes, Mostrar and then Idioma (Language).)

Download translations by going Menu - Bible, Other versions… and ticking the corresponding box. You only need internet while downloading it.
Ticked box=you have this one
Spinner=you're getting it right now
Empty box=you have not downloaded it, or it's deselected.
Select a verse by tapping on it. This brings up some icons top right:
1 Copy (to clipboard)
2 Share (email, SMS…)
3 Compare (see below) and
4 Annotate (see further below)
Compare the verse looking it up in all your installed translations, by tapping the three stacked books.
Annotate by tapping the + sign and:
1 Add a Bookmark (you can group/tag the bookmarks also)
2 Write a note or
3 Highlight the text with some color.

All annotations are visible in all translations, also the ones you install after annotating a verse.
